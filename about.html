ABOUT

Walmart Vriddhi is a supplier development program that aims to train and prepare 50,000 Indian MSMEs to expand their domestic capabilities and participate in the global economy. Participants will be equipped for both online and offline growth, helping them with the tools to potentially become a part of the supply chains of Flipkart and Walmart or those of other domestic and international companies.

Join us to witness Walmart Vriddhi MSME Summit 2022 and celebrate our amazing batch of 15000 MSMEs, who have completed the Walmart Vriddhi Supplier Development Program.
